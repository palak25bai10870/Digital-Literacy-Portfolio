# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
## Overview
This task focuses on understanding cybercrime and promoting awareness about online safety. The objective is to help students recognize common cyber threats and learn practical ways to prevent them.

---

## Contents of the Folder
The folder task-5-cybercrime/ includes:

- casestudy.md / casestudy.pdf  
  A case study on phishing explaining how the attack occurs, who is targeted, and its consequences.

- prevention-checklist.md / .txt  
  A “Stay Safe Online” checklist with actionable safety tips, including financial and UPI-related precautions.

---

## Description of Work

### Case Study
A detailed case study on phishing has been created. It explains:
- What phishing is  
- Step-by-step process of how attacks happen  
- Common targets such as students and professionals  
- Possible consequences including financial loss and data theft  

### Prevention Checklist
A safety checklist has been developed to spread awareness among students. It includes:
- General online safety practices  
- Password and account protection tips  
- UPI and financial safety guidelines  
- Reporting information for cybercrime
