# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
## Overview

This task focuses on understanding common cybercrimes and promoting awareness among students. As a Digital Ambassador, the goal is to educate peers about online threats and provide practical ways to stay safe in the digital world.

## Contents of the Folder

The folder task-5-cybercrime/ includes:

casestudy.md / casestudy.pdf
A detailed case study on a selected cybercrime (Phishing), explaining how it occurs, who is targeted, and its consequences.
prevention-checklist.md / .txt
A “Stay Safe Online” checklist with actionable tips, including UPI and financial safety measures.
## Objectives
To understand different types of cybercrime
To analyze how cyber attacks are carried out
To spread awareness about online safety practices
To encourage responsible digital behavior among students
## Key Learnings
Cybercrimes like phishing can appear very realistic
Lack of awareness is a major reason people fall victim
Simple precautions can prevent major financial and data loss
Reporting cybercrime is important for quick action and prevention
Reporting & Support
🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
📞 Helpline Number: 1930 
## Conclusion

This task enhanced awareness about online threats and highlighted the importance of staying alert while using digital platforms. It also reinforced the role of students in promoting safe and responsible internet usage within their community.
