# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any email or message link
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

  
# UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Double-check UPI IDs and payment details before sending money
- Do not scan random QR codes sent by unknown people
# Report Cybercrime
