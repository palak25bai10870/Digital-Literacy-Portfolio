# Social Media Checklist for College Students


## Do’s:


- Maintain a professional profile and bio
- Think before posting content
- Respect others’ opinions and privacy
- Use privacy settings wisely
- Share useful and positive content

## Don’ts:

- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
- Do not misuse someone else’s content without permission
