# Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
 ## Overview

This task focuses on developing professional communication skills in a digital environment. As a Digital Ambassador, the objective is to understand and demonstrate proper email etiquette and responsible social media behavior.

## Contents of the Folder

The folder task-4-email-etiquette/ includes the following files:

email1.txt / email1.docx
A professional email requesting an assignment deadline extension.
email2.txt / email2.docx
A professional email expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity.
social-media-checklist.md / .txt
A checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage.

## Objectives
To learn and apply professional email writing skills
To understand the importance of tone, structure, and clarity in communication
To promote responsible and ethical use of social media
## Key Learnings
Importance of clear subject lines and proper greetings in emails
Maintaining a polite and professional tone
Structuring emails with clarity and purpose
Awareness of appropriate online behavior and digital responsibility
## Conclusion

This task helped in building essential communication skills required for academic and professional success. It also emphasized the importance of maintaining a positive and responsible digital presence.
