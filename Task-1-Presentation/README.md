# Task-1 Presentation #
I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva, as it is simple to use and offers a variety of attractive templates and design elements. My design focuses on three key aspects: what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. I used clear headings, icons, and short bullet points to make the information easy to understand and visually appealing.

The infographic explains digital literacy as the ability to use digital tools effectively and responsibly. It also highlights useful tools like Google Docs, Canva, and Zoom that help students in learning and completing assignments. In addition, I included safe internet practices such as using strong passwords, avoiding unknown links, and protecting personal information.

While creating the design, I found it interesting to combine visuals with content in a creative way. However, one challenge I faced was keeping the infographic simple while still including all important information without making it look crowded.
